About

iStruktura is an online structural designer for Structural Engineers and Civil Engineering students.

It lets you create, save and access structural designs whether you are on your phone or desktop.

iStruktura was developed by Jerry Marte Jr., a Structural Engineer and a Software Engineer from Cebu, Philippines.
